Commander Chris Hadfield

The coolest guy on Earth and beyond

Commander Chris Hadfield, July 2011:

Noteworthy things about Commander Hadfield:

  • Born and raised in Canada
  • Joined the Canadian Armed Forces and earned an engineering degree at Royal Military College
  • Can fly all types of aircraft
  • Obtained a master's degree in aviation systems from the University of Tennessee Space Institute
  • Became an astronaut in 1992
  • Went on a number of space flights and has spent a considerable number of time in space
  • Has gained popularity due to active social media engagement and due to Space Oddity's cover in space
  • Retired in 2013
  • Since then has been a guest inn several talk shows and social media presentations
  • Most recent endeavour - Generator Arctic
  • Find more about him on his Wikipedia page
